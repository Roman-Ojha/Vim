*) Insert Mode:
    => to go to Insert mode:
        -> i 
    -> O (add new line above and enter into insert mode)
    -> o (add new line and enter into insert mode)
    -> a (go to insert mode and move cursor to next letter)
    -> A (go to insert mode and move cursor to end of line)
    -> ci<letter/symbol> (to remove all the content inside the given symbol like "<content>" and go to insert mode)
